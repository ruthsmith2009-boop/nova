# A2P 10DLC Registration Kit — Sole Proprietor (no LLC/EIN needed)

Registers a phone number to send business texts (missed-call text-back, follow-ups)
so carriers don't filter them as spam. This kit is for **Ruth's NOVA number
(408) 459-4423**, and doubles as the template for registering each future
client's number (every client does their own registration with their own info).

**Approximate Twilio fees** (console shows exact before you confirm):
one-time brand registration around $4, plus a small monthly campaign fee
(a few dollars). Approval usually takes hours to a few days.

---

## Where to go

1. Log in at **console.twilio.com**
2. Left menu: **Messaging → Regulatory Compliance → Onboarding** (also reachable
   via **Trust Hub → A2P Messaging**)
3. Choose **Register for A2P 10DLC** → Brand type: **Sole Proprietor**

## Step 1 — Brand (this is YOU, not the app)

| Field | What to enter |
|---|---|
| Brand / Business name | Ruth Smith (or "AI With Ruth" if it allows a DBA-style name) |
| Business type | Sole Proprietor |
| Country | United States |
| Email | your real email |
| Mobile phone | **your personal cell** — NOT a Twilio number; a one-time code is texted here |
| Address | your real home/business address |
| Vertical/Industry | Professional Services (or Technology) |

Enter the code texted to your cell when prompted.

## Step 2 — Campaign (what the texts are)

| Field | Paste this |
|---|---|
| Use case | **Customer Care** (or "Mixed" if Customer Care isn't offered for sole prop) |
| Campaign description | "This campaign sends customer-service text messages on behalf of a small business: a text-back reply when a customer's inbound call is missed, appointment confirmations, and follow-up replies to customer inquiries. Recipients are customers who called or contacted the business directly." |
| How do recipients opt in? (Message Flow) | "Customers opt in by placing a phone call to the business's published phone number or by providing their phone number directly to the business when requesting service. When a call is missed, the business replies by text to that caller. Every message includes opt-out instructions, and replying STOP ends all messaging." |
| Opt-in type | Verbal / Phone call |
| Sample message 1 | "Hi, this is the AI assistant for AI With Ruth — sorry we missed your call! How can we help? Reply with what you need, or reply BOOK for a consultation. Reply STOP to opt out." |
| Sample message 2 | "Hi [Name], confirming your consultation on [Date] at [Time] with AI With Ruth. Reply YES to confirm or NO to reschedule. Reply STOP to opt out." |
| Subscriber opt-in | Yes |
| Age-gated / Direct lending | No / No |
| Embedded links / phone numbers | Links: No. Phone numbers: No. (Keep it simple for approval; can amend later.) |

## Step 3 — Attach the number

When asked which numbers belong to this campaign, select **(408) 459-4423**.

## Step 4 — Wait for approval

Status shows in Trust Hub. Once **VERIFIED/ACTIVE**, texting from the NOVA
number is carrier-approved and the missed-call text-back feature can go live.

---

## Rules baked into the samples (don't remove these when editing)
- Every marketing-ish text ends with "Reply STOP to opt out" — carrier requirement.
- The AI identifies itself as an AI assistant — California bot-disclosure law.
- Only text people who called you or gave you their number — that's the opt-in
  story carriers approved.

## For future clients
Each client registers their own sole-prop brand (their name, their cell,
their address) — or standard brand if they have an EIN — with their own
client number attached. Copy this kit, swap the business name and samples.
